Q. What time do you arrive to set up?
A. I usually like to arrive 1 hour before the event so I can start setting up.
Q. What type of music will you play at our event?
A. I have music from all genres, including but not limited to that genres best hits, and therefore can play what ever type of music your special occasion requires.
Q. What type of attire will your DJ be wearing?
A. I usually wear at the minumum black slacks, a dress shirt, and I bring with me a sports coat. I do have a tuxedo which I will wear to formal events.
Q. What does your basic equipment include?
A. I use CD players to DJ with, but I also bring a cassette player in case you have anything on tape that you would like me to play. I have enough speakers to cover any size of event from small to large. I also have a few lighting effects (rope lights, disco lights, disco balls, etc...) and a fog machine, but I don't bring them to all shows unless they are needed.
Q. What if we want more lighting?
A. If you need more lighting than what I own, I know of some friends that I can borrow it from to cover your needs or I will buy it before the event if I need to. Either way I will make sure that I have what you want to make your night special.
Q. Do you provide back-up equipment, if so, what?
A. Yes, I do have back up equipment that I can bring if needed. But usually if there is a problem I will know before the event starts, so that I can have it resolved without delaying the event.
Q. If we're not current with the latest artist and titles, can you assist us with our selection?
A. Yes. I can suggest to you current artists/titles from any genre so that you may listen ahead of the event to see if you would like them or not.
