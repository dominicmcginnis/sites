Words from the Heart

This first poem is one I wrote while thinking about the feelings you get when you go to a dance club.  This is a poem I submitted to poetry.com as an entry for a monthly poetry contest.

Lord of the Dance

I sit amongst them in a world of darkness with swirling lights
High above stands their lord on a platform erected to the gods
Spinning and preaching their words of praise
They dance around and howl into the night
As if in some kind of trance and yet as I watch I suddenly feel compelled to join
It is as if the lord on the platform were a wizard casting a mighty spell on those below him
And I one of them, though not a follower cannot resist the call
So I rise and join in the dance
And as I dance I feel at peace, the world flashing by in a swirl of colors
The music giving me clarity and putting me into some kind of trance
Now I understand what it is like to let go
And why it is that they worship him so.

This second poem was one I wrote on my first work assignment away from home.  Its about how being away from my Fiancé made me feel.

A Night Away

I was glad to finally arrive
The night was hot and heavy
But while it was comfortable inside
I couldn't seem to relax
I looked out the window
To view the magical night
It was then I realized
It would not be perfect without you by my side.

I laid down on the bed
All thoughts on you being so distant
That my head was filled with dread
My heart felt so spent filling the void
I tossed and turned an empty shell, dead
Sleep would not take me this annoyed

Gazing in the dark
I could see your face, an angel
In my heart she left her mark
Leaving me filled with peace
And sleep welcomed me.
