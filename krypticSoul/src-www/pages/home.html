Kryptic Soul

If you that want to know in detail who Kryptic Soul is please check out the page on my Personal Information.  For those that are just interested in the small version I will try to sum it up here.  I am a guy who has trouble sharing his emotions in a normal manner, so I write and sing about them.  Because I have problems bearing my soul the message is often cryptic and only understandable to myself after I have bled the pen dry.  For these reasons I have been writing music and poetry for many years now.  I have put together a collection of Poems and Songs into a book that I have given to my family members and I have included here some of those selections.  I have also shared my works with the world by posting on poetry sites, but I wanted to create a central location for all of the creative work that I may do in my life and thus Kryptic Soul was born.

I have gathered here a collection of things going on in my life as well as my poetry.  If you like any of the poetry here feel free to take a copy of it, but if you use it in any professional or personal works please remember to credit me and email me so that I know my work has been appreciated.  However, if you would like a copy of the book I mentioned above please send me an email request and I will get you the information on how you can purchase one.  You can find my email under  contact information on the Personal Information page.  For those of you who have your own poetry sites, please send me an email and I will add your link to my links page.  Also if you have any poetry you would like me to post here send it to me, as I will be adding another page for submitted works of visiting poets.

For all of my friends that want to know about my upcoming wedding I have included the details under the Wedding Information section.  Feel free to check back regularly as I will be posting the details as they develop.
